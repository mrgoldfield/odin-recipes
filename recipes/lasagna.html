Lasagna Recipe

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that is easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes and tomato paste to the skillet. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, layer the meat sauce with lasagna noodles and cheese.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
