Pancakes Recipe

This is a delicious pancakes recipe that is easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 large egg

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, combine the milk, melted butter, and egg.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the griddle.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
